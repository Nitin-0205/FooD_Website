About

OUR MISSION :
Fighting Hunger. Nourishing Our Community.

Our work is guided by these values :
Everyone deserves access to healthy food, where they live, that they can get to. Everyone deserves access to foods that are familiar to them and fit their cultural and dietary needs. Working collaboratively with other community organizations drives our mutual success. Operating in a way that supports equity, sustainability and fairness to all builds stronger communities
